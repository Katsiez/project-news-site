Welcome to the Houm Magazine

An online magazine featuring lifestyle inspiration

The Houm Magazine is an independent online catalogue that features lifestyle inspiration. Through a range of carefully curated articles and images, we aim to inspire you with creating the most enjoyable living, woring and relaxing space in your sanctuary. Browse through our online catalogue for insights and inspiration to help you thrive.

Founded in 2020, when we are met with the new reality of working from home more frequently, The Hour Magazine aims to provide inspiration that is easily achievable and confided by the neutral, earthy tones. Make your house an inspiring sanctuary.
